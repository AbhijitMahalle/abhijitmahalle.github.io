I am a Robotics graduate student at the University of Maryland.

My interest lies in the field of Computer Vision and Deep Learning and I also have a strong background in Mechanical Engineering.

        I have worked on numerous cool projects over the last three months. Few of them are described below:
1. Monocular Human Position Estimator
        I have developed a software program that detects and tracks one or more human beings around a robot using a monocular camera. The software outputs the 3D positon of human beings around a robot so as to maintain a safe distance between itself and human beings while working on the factory floor. You can find the project here.

2. Decluttering Domestic Robot
        I have developed a ROS package of a robot that declutters the house by detecting objects scattered on the house floor, picking them, and placing them at their desired location. I have used a Tiago robot for this purpose. You can find this awesome project here.

3. Mars Rover
        I have created a robot that can collect rock and soil samples from the Mars' surface. It has a Rocker-Bogie mechanism for the suspension system and a 6 DOF KUKA KR5 manipulator attached to the mobile base to collect the samples. You can browse for this project here.
